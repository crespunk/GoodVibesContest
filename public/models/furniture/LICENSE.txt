License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY) — https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Models used: marcusRobot.glb (originally "Drone with Orb.glb")

Note: this is a static (non-rigged) low-poly asset used as a stand-in for a
"floating AI robot" look, not a reproduction of any copyrighted character
design (e.g. Valve's GLaDOS, which was the stylistic reference requested but
is not itself freely licensed). An earlier rigged/skinned model ("Robot Enemy
Flying" by Quaternius) was swapped out after its skeleton failed to render
correctly once it started following the player — this static model avoids
that whole class of skinned-mesh cloning issues.
